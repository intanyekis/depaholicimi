Fan Charged After Rushing Stage at Beyonce and Jay-Z Concert in Atlanta

September 06, 2024
blog

A fan who ran on stage at Beyoncé and Jay-Z‘s Atlanta concert on Saturday, August 25, has been charged with disorderly conduct and simple battery.

Anthony Charles Thomas Maxwell, 26, approached Jay-Z and made contact with him as the couple’s On the Run II concert was ending, the Atlanta Police Department confirmed to Us Weekly.

The man was quickly stopped by security guards and backup dancers, who could be seen running to help the rapper in videos posted on social media by concertgoers.
