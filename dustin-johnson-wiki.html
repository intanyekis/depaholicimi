Dustin Johnson Wiki:- Dustin Johnson, who is also known as Dustin Hunter Johnson, is an American professional golf player.

Dustin has won 2 major championships, the 2016 United States Open Championship at Oakmont Country Club with a 4-under-par score of 276 and the 2020 Masters Tournament with a score of 268, 20-under-par.

He has 6 World Golf Championships victories, with only Tiger Woods has won more, and he is the 1st player to win each of the 4 World Golf Championship events.
