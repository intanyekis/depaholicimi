John Legend, Chrissy Teigen Renew Vows In Italy On 10-Year Anniversary

July 04, 2024
blog

John Legend and Chrissy Teigen celebrated their 10-year anniversary by renewing their vows in Italy over the weekend. The couple tied the knot on Sept. 14, 2013, in Lake Como, Italy — and returned to their wedding destination, PEOPLE reports.

Reportedly, the couple ate at Villa Pizzo, where they held their wedding ceremony a decade ago. A source told the outlet that the celebration was “romantic” and “breathtaking” as they happily “arrived by boat.”

As the couple renewed their vows, a source claimed that they walked out onto a balcony and greeted all the guests. The “flower-filled” celebration included “dancing” and “a lakeside bar,” said the source. “It’s been a very elegant, very classic event.”

According toposts by attendees, Legend wore a white blazer with black detailing, as Teigen donned two gowns by Zuhair Murad. It seems as if Legend also performed for his wife during the event.

Following the vow ceremony, Teigen, 37, revealed that they spent quality time eating Italian food and watching The Office.

On Saturday (Sept. 16), the mother of four shared a photo on Instagram of her laptop playing reruns of the 2005 sitcom.

The food spread included pasta, pizza and a bowl of tomatoes and cheese.

Per PEOPLE, Teigen and Legend first met in 2006 on the set of the singer’s “Stereo” music video. The couple’s first trip together was to Lake Como in 2007, where Teigen said she first realized she wanted to marry her now-husband. 

In 2016, Teigen shared a photo on IG of the couple returning to where they first traveled. She captioned the post, “Back to where it all began – Lake Como, Italy – first came here in 2007. A boat tour guide took us to a little spot on the lake and told us to make a wish. I asked for this to be the man I marry and have children with.”

“I think John asked for the most perfect bite of cacio e pepe,” she joked, adding: “Both came true, and here we are.”
