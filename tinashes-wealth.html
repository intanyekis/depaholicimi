Tinashé Jørgensen Kachingwe Wiki Biography

Tinashe Jorgensen Kachingwe, born February 6, 1993, is an American singer-songwriter, record producer, dancer, actress, and former model. Her name means “God Is With Us” in her father’s native language, Shona, from Zimbabwe. She embarked on her entertainment career at the age of 3, initially modeling and acting. In 2012, Tinashe released two critically acclaimed mixtapes, In Case We Die (2012) and Reverie (2012), which she created in her home studio. Following the release of the mixtapes, she signed with RCA Records. Her third mixtape, Black Water, was released in 2013, followed by her debut solo album, Aquarius, on October 7, 2014. Tinashe’s debut single “2 On,” released in January 2014, reached the top position on the rhythm radio for over 4 weeks and peaked at number 24 on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart, becoming her first Top 40 hit single.

Besides her music career, Tinashe is recognized for her acting. She made her acting debut in the television film Cora Unashamed (2000) and has appeared in various productions, including The Polar Express alongside Tom Hanks. From 2008 to 2009, she had a recurring role in the American sitcom Two and a Half Men as Celeste, Jake’s girlfriend.
