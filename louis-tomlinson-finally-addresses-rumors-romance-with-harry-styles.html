Even before One Direction broke up, fans had already come up with the theory that Louis Tomlinson and Harry Styles had a secret relationship. The bandmates were very close when they worked together, and remained friends even after the band broke up, but far from putting an end to the rumors, ever since they went their separate ways, the speculation only grew.

Neither of them has said much about the rumors, maybe hoping they would go away, but recently Louis Tomlinson was asked directly about it and shared his honest opinion about how the rumors make him feel and how he deals with them.

Louis Tomlinson Addresses Rumors Of A Romance With Harry Styles

Amid One Direction fans, specifically fans of Harry Styles and Louis Tomlinson, there has been an ongoing rumor that the two singers had a relationship a long time ago.

Harry Styles has been a lot more private about his love life, never really giving any specifics about his relationship status or his sexuality, and doesn't seem too bothered about the speculation surrounding him. However, Louis Tomlinson has been more insistent in his denial of the rumors, but it seems he realized the best he can do is ignore them.

"What I realized a few years ago is that there is nothing I can say," he explained. "There is nothing I can do to stop those who believe in this conspiracy. They are so connected to what they believe that they will not see the truth for what it really is."

"I'm sure many people look and find all these little conspiracies that happen in life interesting," he said about the fans. "I'd be lying if I said it didn't irritate me a little, but it's the nature of the job."

That doesn't mean there aren't moments when the rumors don't get on his nerves, as he doesn't want the people close to him reading false theories about his personal life.

"There are times when it gets very personal. I have my son, Freddie. He is the most important person in my life. And occasionally, [these rumors] end up addressing things that are a little unfair."

Still, he prefers to let it go. "This is what we have now. There's nothing I can do about it. Nothing I can say to stop people from inventing what they want to invent. So, so be it."

Louis Tomlinson Finds The Rumors Disrespectful

While the One Direction alum doesn't normally like to talk about the rumors, this is not the first time he's shared how the speculations make him feel. A few years ago, he called the rumors about him and Harry Styles "disrespectful" to his girlfriend at the time, and said it even affected the way he interacted with his friend.

"I’ve never actually been asked about it directly. It’s a funny thing. It kind of happened naturally for me and Harry because a certain amount of the fans drew up this conspiracy. When it first came around I was with Eleanor, and it actually felt a little bit disrespectful to Eleanor, who is my girlfriend now," Louis Tomlinson said in 2017.

He also said that it created a bit of tension between him and Harry Styles. "I’m so protective over things like that, about the people I love. So it created this atmosphere between the two of us where everyone was looking into everything we did. It took away the vibe you get off anyone. It made everything a little bit more unapproachable. I think [what’s happened since] shows that it was never anything 'real' if I can use that word."
