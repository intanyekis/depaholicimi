Ronda Rousey Talks WWE Return

Ronda Rousey was recently a guest on The MMA Hour, and the subject of working with WWE came up.

In regards to returning to the company following her much-publicized Wrestlemania 31 appearance, Rousey said (to MMA Hour, via MMAFighting.com) “I’m going to find a way. Somehow, some way, some day. I just have no clue when.”

She said her busy schedule and UFC contract is preventing her from returning sooner like she wants, but she said it will happen, but down the road. Ronda Rousey added:

“I’m willing to find a way to get back in there. I don’t know how it would be possible in the foreseeable future. It’s definitely not possible for a very, very long time, I have an extremely full schedule.”
