Trent Tomlinson (born July 3, 1975) is a country music singer from the United States. Tomlinson was signed to Lyric Street Records in 2005 after many unsuccessful efforts to obtain a record contract. His first album, Country Is My Rock, was released in early 2006. On the Billboard Hot Country Songs charts in the United States, this album produced three Top 40 singles: “Drunker Than Me” at No. 19, “One Wing in the Fire” at No. 11, and “Just Might Have Her Radio On” at No. 21. In 2009, three more singles were released.
