Angelina Jolie is a talented actress, but motherhood is her first priority. The Hollywood talent is a mother of six, who shares Maddox, Pax, Zahara, Shiloh, and twins Vivienne and Knox with ex-husband Brad Pitt. After being married for two years, the couple split in 2016 and went to court to work out a custody arrangement. Brad was awarded joint custody in May 2021. Since the separation, the pair’s legal battle has continued, including figuring out ownership of their shared winery.
