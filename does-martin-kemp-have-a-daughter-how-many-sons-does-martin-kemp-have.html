Does Martin Kemp have a daughter? How many sons does Martin Kemp have?

September 06, 2024
blog

Martin Kemp and Shirlie Holliman have two children together. Their daughter, Harley Moon Kemp, was born in 1989 and is a photographer, director, and singer-songwriter.

Martin Kemp has worked on campaigns as a photographer and has written a couple of songs on her parents’ latest album, In The Swing Of It. Harley Moon Kemp is the first child of Martin Kemp and Shirlie Holliman.

Their son, Roman Kemp, was born in 1993 and is a radio DJ and TV presenter. He has presented shows such as The X Factor and The Hot Desk.

Roman Kemp is best known for his radio DJ gig at Capital FM and last year even headed Down Under to take part in I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here!.

Martin Kemp hosted TV’s 50 Hardest Men for Sky1 in July 2008 and guest-starred as Mr. Burley in the BBC drama series Waterloo Road. Kemp hosted the first series of “Martin Kemp’s Murder Files”.

Martin Kemp was announced as the thirteenth celebrity to participate in the summer series of Celebrity Big Brother 2012 and was placed third on 7 September 2012.
