Jamie Bamber is a British actor, producer, stage artist, and voice actor who is known for his television roles like playing DS Matt Devlin in the ITV police procedural legal drama series Law & Order: UK, Lee ‘Apollo’ Adama in the military science fiction series Battlestar Galactica, Dr. Matt Kendal in the drama series Peak Practice, Dr. Tyler Wilson in the TNT medical drama series Monday Mornings, DI Tim Williamson in the nordic noir detective series Marcella, Kev Allison in the drama series The Smoke, Colonel Alexander Coltrane in the action-adventure spy-drama series Strike Back, Sam Wright in the anthology series Innocent, Cpt. Dennis “Dotsy” Doheny in the action drama series Ultimate Force, and Jake Malloy in the police procedural series NCIS. Jamie Bamber has also been cast in various movies such as Pulse 2: Afterlife, Before I Sleep, Tom and Jerry: Robin Hood and His Merry Mouse, Filly Brown, John Doe: Vigilante, and Titanic 666.

    Jamie Bamber Facts

  • He lived in Paris for a few of his childhood years and is well-versed in Italian and French.
  • Jamie Bamber provided vocals to the character of Robin Hood in the 2012 animated direct-to-video action-adventure musical comedy film Tom and Jerry: Robin Hood and His Merry Mouse.
  • In 2012, he played Dr. Michael Hathaway (a handsome hotshot behavioral science professor recruited at CLMU) in the police procedural crime drama television series Perception.
  • He appeared as Paul in the 2013 drama film Before I Sleep, starring David Warner, Tom Sizemore, Bonnie Wright, Cynthia Gibb, Eric Roberts, and Eugene Simon.
  • Alongside Kellan Lutz, Jesse Williams, Jess Weixler, and Lucia Guerrero, Jamie Bamber co-starred as Sean in the 2016 crime thriller film Money.
  • He brought to life the character of DCI Martyn Hunter in the police procedural television series DI Ray in 2022.
  • Jamie Bamber has been best friends with Ioan Gruffudd.
  • In 2022, he co-starred as Professor Hal Cochran in the supernatural horror film Titanic 666.
  • He enjoys rugby, golf, horseback riding, skiing, dancing, and rap music.
