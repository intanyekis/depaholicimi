Reviewing the Best Keratin Hair Serums of 2024

1

Vitamins Hair Cosmetics Kertain Hair Serum

Pros

  • Protects hair from sun damage
  • Creates fuller, thicker hair
  • Provides protein repair
  • Suitable for all hair types

Cons

  • Fragrance may be too strong for some

The VITAMINS hair cosmetics Keratin Hair Serum is a revolutionary product designed to deliver salon-quality results. Featuring a luxurious blend of Moroccan argan and coconut oil, this serum provides instant thickness and fullness to the hair. It also helps to revive, restore, and repair dry and damaged hair with collagen repairing and hydrating therapy. Plus, the serum offers incredible strengthening benefits and thermal heat protection, making it perfect for those who regularly use flat irons, blow dryers, or curling irons. The included biotin and castor oil provide nourishment and moisture to the hair without making it greasy or weighed down. Perfect for all hair types — especially frizzy, curly, extra thick, or fine hair — this serum helps to define curls and tame flyaways while providing amazing anti-humidity benefits. The formula is even water-based and sulfate-free to ensure weightlessness and volumizing bounce. Plus, it is alcohol-free, paraben-free, and cruelty-free, and comes in a compact size that is easy to carry with you whenever you travel, making it our top choice overall.

2

GK HAIR Kertain Hair Serum

Pros

  • Supports health scalp
  • Provides hydration
  • Helps tame frizzy and brittle hair
  • Prevent further damage

Cons

  • Bottle size is small for price

GK HAIR’s Keratin Hair Serum is a powerful and nourishing product designed to invigorate hair and create smoother, fuller, and healthier locks. It’s formulated with a combination of argan oil and keratin, two powerful ingredients that provide a protective layer around each strand. The salon-like formula also helps to tame frizz and brittle hair, while sealing in moisture and providing additional protection against environmental factors. Anyone can use this product whether their hair is straight, wavy, curly, coiled, or tightly coiled. Its lightweight feel makes it easy to apply on clean dry or damp hair after shampooing and conditioning by working it through the ends up to mid-length to help tame flyaways and frizz. The serum also provides heat protection from heat tools like blow dryers and curling irons, making it a great choice for everyday styling.

3

L Luseta Kertain Hair Serum

Pros

  • Nourish damaged hair
  • Works on dry or towel-dried hair
  • Safe for color-treated hair
  • Free of sulfates, parabens, and gluten

Cons

  • May make your hair greasy

The Luseta Keratin Hair Serum is an excellent product for anyone who has dry and damaged hair. The unique keratin oil-infused formula nourishes and strengthens the hair, leaving it feeling strong, healthy, and shiny. The serum is also weightless and helps to resist breakage while adding softness and shine. When using this product, you can apply it to dry hair for conditioning of the ends, frizz management, and shine. You can also use it on towel-dried hair by placing a few drops into your palms or fingertips and applying it to the mid-to-end sections/ The serum is completely color-safe and free of sulfates, parabens, and gluten, so it won’t damage or fade your hair or your dye in any way. Luseta offers a great way to restore natural beauty to your locks without harsh and potentially damaging chemical treatments.

4

Remilia Hair Kertain Hair Serum

Pros

  • Easy to use
  • Comes in travel-friendly capsules
  • Free from parabens or sulfates
  • Help reduce frizziness, split ends, and breakage

Cons

  • More expensive than other options

The Keratin Hair Serum from Remilia Hair is an effective and easy-to-use product that can strengthen, protect, and repair damaged hair. This hair treatment contains plant-based capsules that are rich in vitamins and proteins that help reduce frizz, breakage, tangles, and split ends after just one use. While hydrating the hair, it also acts as a scalp protector, unlocking your hair’s shine and softness while promoting stronger and thicker hair growth. The formulation of this shampoo also works as a heat protectant, preventing damage to the scalp and hair via exposure to the sun or styling tools. It leaves your hair non-greasy and smelling fresh all day long, without any need for added styling products. Plus, it works for all types of hair — long or short, fine or thick, straight or curly — and is even safe for chemically-treated hair.

5

Keranique Kertain Hair Serum

Pros

  • Repairs damaged hair
  • Improve thickness and volume
  • Nourish the scalp and hair follicles
  • Contains natural ingredients

Cons

  • A bit pricey for the bottle size

The Keranique Keratin Hair Serum is a unique solution for hair regrowth and hair loss prevention. Developed by experts to improve scalp health and nourish the hair follicles, this serum helps reduce excess sebum and DHT on the scalp, which are major contributors to hair loss. The serum contains a blend of peptides, stem cell culture extract, vitamins, and botanicals that help nourish the scalp and hair follicles for healthier, fuller-looking hair. When used in connection with Keranique’s anti-thinning shampoos and volumizing conditioners, the Follicle Boosting Serum helps reinforce, protect, and repair thinning hair with its Keratin Amino Complex protein, adding volume and thickness to each strand while nourishing each follicle. The serum also contains natural ingredients such as Red Clover, Panax Ginseng Root, Burdock Root, Carob Seed, and Carrageen Moss extracts that offer additional nourishment.
