Who is ZHC?

ZHC is an American social media personality. He is known for his amazing comical arts. He also uploads tutorials on YouTube on his YouTube channel.

ZHC stands for Zach Hsieh Comic Art.

ZHC: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

He was born Zachary Hsieh on 8 Jan 1999 in New Jose, California, United States. Currently, he is 22 years old.

His father’s, mother’s, and siblings’ identities are not disclosed.

His ancestors are from China.

Education

ZHC: Professional Life, Career

ZHC is a very popular comical artist. His amazing sketches of different characters have attracted several fans. He loves to tell stories using pictures as well as drawing superheroes and buildings.

He left everything to make his dream come true as an artist in 2015. Now he owns an organization named Art Alliance and the Copic Abuse Foundation.

He also helps others by uploading tutorial videos about sketches and sharing aspects of his life in short vlogs and talks.

ZHC also has a YouTube channel named zhcomicart. This channel was made on 7 Aug 2013 and has over 5.97 million subscribers. In it, he uploads challenges, compilations, and vlogs.

His video titled Customizing 11 iPhone 11s, Then Giving Them To People!! has got more than 33 million views. This video was uploaded on Oct 1, 2019.
