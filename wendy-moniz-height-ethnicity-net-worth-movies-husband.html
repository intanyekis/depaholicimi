Wendy Moniz is an American television actress who is known for roles as Laura Moretti in House of Cards (2015–2016) and as Governor Lynelle Perry in Yellowstone (2018–present). She attended and graduated from Siena College with a major in English and Theater. She landed a role in the primetime CBS drama pilot Partners, in 1999. In 2005, she guest starred in an episode of the NBC legal drama Law & Order.

Wendy Moniz Ethnicity

Wendy Moniz is of Portuguese and Irish descent. She was born in Kansas City, Missouri on January 19, 1969. She is 54 years old, as of 2023. She attended B.M.C.Durfee High School Fall River, Massachusetts. She is also known by name, Wendy Moniz-Grillo.

Wendy Moniz Husband

Wendy Moniz Career Timeline

From 1995 to 1999, Moniz made her television debut in the CBS daytime soap opera Guiding Light as Dinah Marler. She landed a role in the primetime CBS drama pilot Partners, in 1999. She was cast as a series regular in the role of Rachel McCabe in the sixth and final season of the CBS series Nash Bridges, in 2000. In 2012, she also appeared in the ABC series 666 Park Avenue.

Moniz was cast as lead in the independent drama film The Grief of Others directed by Patrick Wang, in 2014. She has played Governor Lynelle Perry in the Paramount Network drama series Yellowstone, since 2018.
